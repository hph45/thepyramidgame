RULES


1. Everyone pays $1 to join.


2. Everyone joins a corporation. If a referral code isn't specified, the lowest valued one will be joined.


3. You cannot switch corporations once you've joined.


4. 60% of the entrance fee goes to your corporation's valuation. 40% is lost.


5. To takeover another corporation, your corporation must have 2.5x their valutation.


6. Anyone in the corporation can initiate a takeover if the conditions are met.


7. It takes 12 hours for a takeover to complete, if the conditions are not met at anytime during those six hours, the takeover is canceled.


8. You must wait two days in between takeovers.


9. If your corporation is taken over, you lose and cannot rejoin until the start of the next round.


10. After the round is over, everyone in the corporation splits the valuation based off of their seniority and contributions.
